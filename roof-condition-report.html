What It Is

A Clear Roof Condition Snapshot—From Above

Because What You Can't See, Can Cost You

A SkySure Roof Condition Report is a drone-based visual report that documents observable roof conditions over time. It’s designed for homeowners, buyers, sellers, and real estate professionals who want clarity and a documented roof history.

  • High-resolution aerial imagery
  • Observations of visible wear, damage, or changes
  • Date-stamped visual documentation
  • A clear, easy-to-understand summary
Important: Reports document observable conditions only and are not a licensed home or roofing inspection.

FAQs

Roof Condition Report Questions

Lake Norman + Charlotte

  • Is this a home inspection?

    No. Reports document observable conditions only and are not a licensed home or roofing inspection.

  • Do I need to be home?

    Usually no. We just need access to the exterior and a safe place to launch/land.

  • How do reminders work?

    You pick annual, semi-annual, or quarterly. SkySure will contact you when it’s time for your next report.

  • Can I cancel anytime?

    Yes. No long-term contracts.
